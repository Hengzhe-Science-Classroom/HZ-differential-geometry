# Differential Geometry · Interactive

**Curves, surfaces, and manifolds**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 5 · Research Frontier

## What You'll Learn

- Curves: curvature, torsion, Frenet-Serret
- Surfaces: first and second fundamental forms
- Gauss-Bonnet theorem
- Smooth manifolds and tangent spaces
- Riemannian metrics and connections
- Geodesics and curvature

## References

- Manfredo P. do Carmo, *Differential Geometry of Curves and Surfaces* (2nd ed., Dover)
- John M. Lee, *Introduction to Smooth Manifolds* (2nd ed., GTM 218, Springer)
- Manfredo P. do Carmo, *Riemannian Geometry* (Birkhäuser)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
